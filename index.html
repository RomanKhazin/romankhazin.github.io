Toronto Childcare Spaces

Python project exploring and analyzing data from the city regarding availability of childcare spaces in the city. The data is broken down by age group, availibility of subsidies, and postal code. The results are then visualized in Tableau, available in the link below.

Toronto Homeless Shelters

SQL Project exploring the usage and availability of homeless shelters in Toronto in the first half of 2021. After cleaning up the data and filling in null values, I looked at differences between various types of shelters and focused in on outliers. The results of the project are visualized in a pair of Tableau dashboards, available in the link below.

Toronto Short-Term Rentals

Python data exploration project looking at short-term rental registrations in Toronto. The data is summarized by political wards and by postal codes and further analyzed on a per capita basis and in relation to the total number of dwellings. The data is then visualized using matplotlib.
